About Nounou-dit-tout


Nounou-dit-tout is the result of my own reflection and solitary work. The idea came to me while I was searching for an innovative and useful application. I aimed to simplify the lives of parents and caregivers by enabling them to save time in managing invoices, exchanging information, conversations, and photos.

This application was developed as part of a portfolio project for Holberton School, where I was able to put my web development skills into practice.
